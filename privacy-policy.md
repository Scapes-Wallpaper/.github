# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-07-02

This Privacy Policy explains how Scapes ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Scapes application, web portals, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Scapes, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### 1.1 Account and profile information
When you register as a contributor or admin, we may collect:
- name or display name
- email address
- password (stored in a hashed and secured form)
- role or account type
- profile-related information you choose to provide

### 1.2 Content and upload information
If you upload wallpapers or related content, we may collect:
- uploaded files
- file metadata such as filename, size, type, and resolution
- title, description, category, and tags
- moderation status and review history
- timestamps of uploads, edits, and deletions

### 1.3 Usage and analytics data
We may collect information about how you use the Service, including:
- search queries and keywords
- viewed, downloaded, and applied wallpapers
- clicks, navigation behavior, and session activity
- recommendations and categories interacted with
- device information, browser type, IP address, and approximate location
- logs related to errors, authentication, and system performance

### 1.4 Local application data
The desktop application may store local configuration such as:
- download folder location
- selected wallpaper source
- application preferences and settings
- locally stored wallpaper files and metadata

If you provide a personal API key for third-party wallpaper providers, that key may be stored locally in the application settings for your convenience and used only to access the selected provider on your behalf.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- provide, operate, and improve the Service
- allow users to search, download, and apply wallpapers
- enable contributor accounts to upload, manage, and track wallpaper content
- allow admins to review, approve, reject, and moderate submissions
- authenticate users and protect accounts from unauthorized access
- send account-related communications such as login, password reset, or moderation notifications
- analyze usage patterns to improve features, recommendations, and search experience
- detect abuse, spam, fraud, and violations of our rules
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies

## 3. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

### 3.1 With service providers
We may share information with trusted service providers that help us host, store, analyze, notify, or secure the Service, such as hosting providers, email services, analytics tools, and cloud storage providers.

### 3.2 With third-party wallpaper providers
When you use wallpaper sources such as Pexels, Unsplash, Pixabay, or other external services, we may request content and metadata from those providers in accordance with their terms and privacy policies. We may also use your search or source selection data to improve the experience and service performance.

### 3.3 With admins and moderators
Content submitted by contributors may be visible to admins and moderators for review and moderation purposes.

### 3.4 For legal reasons
We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Scapes, users, or the public.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain personal and operational data for as long as necessary to:
- provide the Service
- comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- resolve disputes
- enforce our agreements
- maintain security and audit records

In general:
- account data is retained while the account remains active or until you request deletion
- uploaded content and moderation records are retained for as long as needed for platform operation and legal compliance
- analytics and logs may be retained in aggregated or anonymized form for longer periods
